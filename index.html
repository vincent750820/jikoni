Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef Wanjiru Mwangi, Jikoni started as a small eatery in Nairobi's bustling city center. Our name, which means "kitchen" in Swahili, reflects our commitment to creating a home-like dining experience.

We pride ourselves on using traditional cooking methods while incorporating modern culinary techniques to bring you the best of Kenyan cuisine. All our ingredients are sourced from local farmers and markets to support our community and ensure freshness.
